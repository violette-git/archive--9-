How to Play Bunny Snake

Controls:

↑ ↓ ← → or W A S D to move your bunny

P to pause/resume the game

Game Objectives:

  • Guide your bunny to collect carrots
  • Each carrot adds 10 points to your score
  • Advance to the next level every 100 points
  • Higher levels increase game speed and enemy frequency

Enemies:

Fox - Moves at moderate speed

Wolf - Faster and more dangerous

Enemies will cut your bunny's tail if they cross it!

You lose 5 points for each segment cut off.

If an enemy hits your bunny's head, it's game over!

Quick Tips:

Use arrow keys or WASD to move your bunny snake.

Collect carrots to grow longer and earn points!

Avoid hitting the walls or yourself.

Watch out for foxes and wolves! They will cut your bunny's tail if they cross it.

Press P to pause/resume the game.
